Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2024

Introduction

DateGenie ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application DateGenie (the "App").

Information We Do NOT Collect

DateGenie is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device information
  • Usage analytics
  • User preferences or settings
  • Any data about your date activities or choices

Local Data Storage

The App may store certain information locally on your device, such as:

  • Your favorite date ideas
  • App preferences and settings
  • Scratch card progress

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

DateGenie does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The App operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to function.

App Store Data

When you download DateGenie from the App Store, Apple may collect certain information as described in their Privacy Policy. We do not have access to this information.

Children's Privacy

DateGenie is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not have specific concerns about children's privacy. However, we recommend parental guidance for users under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Developer: Shahab Geravesh
  • App: DateGenie
  • Available through the App Store support system

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including but not limited to:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

By using DateGenie, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
